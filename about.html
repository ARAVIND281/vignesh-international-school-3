About Vignesh International School


Vignesh International School is sponsored by Sree Selva Vinayagar trust. The Trust was started in the year 1993, with a noble aim of imparting quality education to the rural publicin and around Tiruvannamalai district. This Trust started the VIGNESH POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE in the year 1995 and has been successfully running the institution with a good academic record. VIGNESH TEACHER TRAINING INSTITUTE, VIGNESH COLLEGE OF EDUCATION AND VIGNESH NURSING COLLEGE are other institutions run by the trust. The Trust is a progressive Trust with fifteen years of experience and is a pioneer in providing quality education. The role of a pioneer in any field of knowledge is to lead the way and change the conventional wisdom for the greater good. To bring about this change, the Trust has started Vignesh International School.

Vignesh International School has been established to cater to the growing international demand for high quality education in the context of rapidly globalizing educational scenario. The school is located in a serene atmosphere on Manalurpet road at Melputhiyandal village in the holy town of Tiruvannamalai. It is at a distance of 7 kms. from the central bus stand.

our mission


Vignesh International School, Tiruvannamalai enriches each student with a quality, holistic education besides providing the social, emotional facets of child development, a zeal for life long learning and a passion for personal excellence within a supportive and warm environment.

our vision


"Linking Learning to Life"


The School aims to make learning a creative and enjoyable experience. Through a blend of nationally and globally acclaimed educational opportunities, the school seeks to encourage children to reach their full potential while discovering themselves. The accent is on developing innovative and critical thinking skills, compassion, respect for one's own culture and a global outlook.
